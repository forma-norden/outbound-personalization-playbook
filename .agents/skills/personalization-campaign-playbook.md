# personalization-campaign-playbook

Use this skill to determine when you should invest in 1:1 personalization
versus when you should use broad segmentation, effectively allocating
your team's time and resources.

## The Effort vs. Value Matrix

Not every prospect deserves 10 minutes of research.
Segment your total addressable market (TAM) into three tiers.

### Tier 1: The "Whales" (Top 5-10%)
- **Definition:** Enterprise accounts with massive ACV, or specific strategic targets you *must* win.
- **Approach:** Deep 1:1 Personalization (Manual or deeply assisted AI).
- **Execution:** SDR researches 3+ data buckets. Crafts a highly customized email thread, potentially involving video or direct mail.
- **Volume:** 5-10 accounts per week, per rep.

### Tier 2: The "Dolphins" (Middle 30-40%)
- **Definition:** Solid ICP fit, average deal size, clear buying indicators.
- **Approach:** Segmented Relevance + AI Personalization at Scale.
- **Execution:** Group by strong commonalities (e.g., "SaaS companies using Hubspot that recently hired a CMO"). Use automated AI tools to pull one relevant hook.
- **Volume:** 50-150 accounts per week, per rep.

### Tier 3: The "Minnows" (Bottom 50%)
- **Definition:** Below average ACV, transactional sales motion, or broad TAM with no distinct specific signals.
- **Approach:** Broad Segmentation only. No 1:1 personalization.
- **Execution:** highly relevant industry-level messaging. "Because you are a [Job Title] in [Industry], you likely struggle with [Pain]."
- **Volume:** 500+ accounts per week via automated sequences.

## Personalization vs. Segmentation

**Personalization:** "I saw your company won the Best Workplace award in Austin." (About them specifically, hard to scale without AI).
**Segmentation:** "Most VP Engineering leaders in Fintech right now are struggling with SOC2 compliance." (About their group, infinitely scalable).

**Rule of Thumb:** If your ACV is under $10,000, do not manually personalize. Rely entirely on Tier 2 AI automation and Tier 3 strict segmentation.

## Output Contract
When asked for a campaign strategy, provide:
- The recommended Tier mix based on the user's ACV and TAM size
- Examples of the personalization vs. segmentation approach for their specific product
- A capacity model outlining how many accounts an SDR can process under this structure per week
