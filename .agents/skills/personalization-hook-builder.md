# personalization-hook-builder

Use this skill to convert raw research data into a compelling first line
(the "hook") that drives the reader into your pitch.

## The Anatomy of a Perfect Hook

A strong hook does three things:
1. **Validates attention:** Proves you aren't a generic bot.
2. **Defines the premise:** Sets up the problem you are going to solve.
3. **Is shockingly brief:** Never more than 15-20 words.

## Strong Hooks vs. Lite Hooks

### Strong Hooks (Premise-Driven)
Focuses on a high-intent signal (Buckets 1, 2, 4 from the research framework) and directly connects to your value prop.
*   **Template:** "Saw [Observation]. Usually when companies do this, they struggle with [Pain]."
*   **Example:** "Saw you're aggressively hiring SDRs right now. Usually, ramp time becomes the biggest bottleneck when adding headcount that fast."

### Lite Hooks (Rapport-Driven)
Focuses on lower-intent signals (Buckets 3, 5, 6) just to prove human effort, then pivots hard to the pitch.
*   **Template:** "Noticed [Observation]. Reaching out because [Pitch]."
*   **Example:** "Noticed you spent 4 years at Stripe before joining Acme. Reaching out because Acme fits the exact profile for our new billing API."

## The "Observation + Transition" Formula

Never leave an observation hanging. It must transition into why you are emailing them *today*.

**Bad:** "Saw your post about AI. It was really insightful. I want to sell you my tool."
**Good:** "Saw your post about AI data privacy. Since you're focused on securing LLM inputs, wanted to share how we handle PII masking..."

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- **The "Great Profile" Lie:** "I was checking out your LinkedIn profile and was really impressed by your background." (Everyone knows this is fake).
- **The Book Report:** Summarizing their entire career in paragraph one.
- **The Bait and Switch:** "Go Yankees! Anyway, need a new CRM?" (No logical transition).

## Output Contract
When asked to write a hook based on research, provide:
- 2 variations of a Strong Hook (if data supports it)
- 1 variation of a Lite Hook
- The exact transition sentence connecting the hook to the user's value prop
